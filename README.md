# Bellabeat Case Study

## Case Study Overview

This is a case study for the completion of the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate (V2). Bellabeat is a high-tech company focused of manufacturing health products meant for women. 

## The Google Analysis Process.
### Phase 1. Ask, Main Assingment 

Bellabeat is a madium sized company that would love to reach a larger audience and become a big player in the women health abd fitness industry. The executive and marketing team wants me to analyze the compay's smart devices' data to gain insight into how consumers are using them. The insights I discover will help guide marketing strategy for the company

#### Hypothesis
1. People who monitor their weight have good health indicators like good average sleep in a given period of time
2. People who are active tend to sleep better.
3. There is a positive correlation between ativity level and burning calories.

### Phase 2. Prepare
#### Data Source
The dataset used in this project is a public dataset called **FitBit Fitness Tracker Data**. Made available through the account **Mobius** on Kaggle. The data was collected using a Fitbit device from 30 participants who consented to the use of data from their devices. The .csv files I used from the dataset are as follows:
1. weightLogInfo_merged.csv
2. sleepDay_merged.csv
3. dailyActivity_merged.csv

#### Data Limitations
1. The data was collected from 30 participants who will represent the whole up market segment that Bellbeat seeks to serve.
2. It was collected in a period of two months.
